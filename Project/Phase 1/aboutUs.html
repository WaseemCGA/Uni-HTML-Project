    • about us

    A page was created to reduce stress by university students with the aim of providing a supportive environment that helps alleviate the psychological pressures students face during their studies. Stress is a common issue that affects both academic performance and mental health. Through this page, students aim to share their experiences and discuss effective strategies for managing stress, such as deep breathing techniques and meditation. The page also offers educational resources and tools to promote overall well-being. The ultimate goal is to create a supportive community that enhances the overall university experience for students.
